Services

Data Analysis and Reporting

I specialize in analyzing large datasets to uncover trends, patterns, and actionable insights that can drive informed business decisions. I create customized reports and dashboards using tools like Looker Studio, Tableau, and Power BI, ensuring that businesses have access to clear, understandable data. Additionally, I focus on monitoring and optimizing key performance indicators (KPIs), such as conversion rates, customer lifetime value, and sales metrics, to provide actionable insights that enhance business performance.

Dashboard Design and Development

I design and develop dynamic, interactive dashboards tailored to visualize key data points and support strategic decision-making. By automating data integration and updates, I ensure that reports are current and provide real-time insights. My goal is to create dashboards that are not only informative but also user-friendly, empowering teams to easily access and understand important business metrics.

Learn more

E-commerce Analytics

With extensive experience working with e-commerce platforms like Shopify, Amazon, and Meta Ads Manager, I offer deep analysis into sales data and customer behavior. I measure the effectiveness of digital marketing campaigns using tools such as Google Analytics and Ads Managers, and provide valuable insights into inventory levels, sales performance, and customer trends. My goal is to help businesses maximize their digital presence and drive growth through data-driven marketing strategies.

Financial Modeling

I create detailed financial models to analyze business performance and forecast future trends. These models provide clear insights into financial performance, highlighting areas of growth and potential risks. I develop comprehensive financial reports that enable businesses to track emerging trends and make data-driven decisions for future success.

Business Strategy Support

I work closely with businesses to identify opportunities for growth by analyzing data and providing actionable recommendations. Whether optimizing sales performance, improving customer retention, or streamlining operations, my data-driven approach helps businesses gain a competitive edge and achieve long-term success.

Spreadsheet Solutions

My goal is to provide businesses with streamlined, efficient solutions that make data manipulation and reporting easier, more accurate, and more insightful.

About me



I’m a dedicated Data Analyst with four years of freelancing expertise in the dynamic world of e-commerce. My passion lies in transforming complex data into actionable insights that drive growth and success for businesses.


With hands-on experience in tools like Looker Studio, Shopify, Google Analytics, and Meta Ads Manager, I specialize in crafting dynamic dashboards and reports that empower teams to make data-driven decisions with confidence. My work uncovers trends, optimizes marketing strategies, and enhances performance metrics, from sales and customer behavior to inventory management.


Known for my ability to work independently and collaboratively, I thrive on tackling challenges and delivering precise, impactful results. Whether it's analyzing sales trends, creating financial models, or fine-tuning KPIs, I’m not just an analyst—I’m your partner in unlocking potential and achieving success.
